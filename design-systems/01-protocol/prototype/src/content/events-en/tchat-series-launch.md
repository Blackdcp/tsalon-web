## Why T Chat began

What are engineering teams inside major technology companies working on? What do experienced engineers pay attention to? T Chat invited people doing the work to answer those questions in public.

## The format

Each episode paired a 30-minute guest presentation with a 30-minute conversation. After the livestream, the guest, topic, video and wider series remained part of a public community archive.
